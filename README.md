This repository contains the replication code and materials for the paper:

> **"Market Development and Diversification in Labeled Bond Markets: Evidence from the IFC Green Bond Dataset"**  
> *Chaitanya Venkateswaran*, Geneva Graduate Institute  
> Fall 2025

The project studies how the concentration of sovereign bond maturities (tenor), the share of local currency issuance, and tenure of issuance relationships evolve over time and across countries, and how these features relate to market development and stability.
